Advanced Research Project in Statistics

"Comparison of Phylogenetic Comparative Methods to Investigate Senescence in Ectothermic Tetrapods"

This is my advanced research project in statistics, completed in my final year of university. In this project, I test and compare two phylogenetic comparative methods to regular OLS regression to answer hypothesis related to aging and longevity in ectothermic tetrapods. R was used to run all analyses with the use of several R packages such as “phytools” “ape”, "caper" and “geiger”.
